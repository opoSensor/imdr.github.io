Smart portable opioid biosensors

Brief Introduction

Our project, “An Integrated and Miniaturized Opioid Sensor System: Advancing Evidence-Based Strategies for Addressing the Opioid Crisis,” was awarded a one-year, $649,585 U.S. National Science Foundation (NSF) Convergence Accelerator Phase 1 grant aligned to the program's Track L: Real-World Chemical Sensing Applications

Key functionalities include:

  • Precise OPOID Drugs Detection

  • High-functional Sensors

  • Smart User-friendly UI Design
